WHAT IS ANALYSIS AND ANALYTICS?

In the realm of data analysis, a diverse array of data types is encountered. Effectively dissecting data into manageable segments serves to enhance comprehension. While addressing fundamental questions of "What," "Why," and "How," the focal point of analytics lies in forecasting future occurrences rather than scrutinizing past events. The exploration of potential future scenarios necessitates the application of logical and computational reasoning to the components derived during the analysis process. By employing this strategic approach, data assumes a pivotal role in generating valuable insights to facilitate informed predictions of future events.
